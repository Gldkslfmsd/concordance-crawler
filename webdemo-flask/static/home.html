Welcome to ConcordanceCrawler Web Demo!

ConcordanceCrawler is a tool for automatic concordance extraction from the Internet. What is a concordance? It's a sentence containing some given word. Take an English word, and ConcordanceCrawler will be able to download you hundreds of thousands of English sentences containing your word.

Why?

A big source of concordances is very useful for computational linguists, with its help they can improve many linguistic applications, for example machine translation.

What is this page?

This is a web demonstration page. Via this link you can submit a new crawling job, browse submitted jobs, see how does ConcordanceCrawler work and download its output.

Here you can download some big example corpora extracted by ConcordanceCrawler.

What next?

You can download ConcordanceCrawler from PyPI, install it and enjoy. You can also visit its GitHub. If you're not satisfied with its features (or bugs), you can contact its author, report a bug or an issue. You can also help yourself :)

ConcordanceCrawler is also a library. You can adapt it by your own wishes, you can e.g. change its filtering of Non-English sentences to your own language, change the source of links and much more. Enjoy!
